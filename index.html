Why DudukPro?

Most duduk players practise with a patchwork of generic apps: one tuner, one metronome, one audio tool, plus random online advice for instrument care and exercises.

DudukPro brings everything into one calm place, designed purely for duduk.

Understands the duduk

Reed calibration and duduk-specific keys instead of “just any note on a piano”.

Practice-ready tools

Tuner, drones, metronome, exercises and care – all in one app.

Audio lab for real music

Slow down, trim, transpose and loop phrases from the recordings you love.

The tuner knows the duduk – and your reed.

Tune quickly with a fast, stable tuner tuned to the duduk. Use reed calibration when your reed sits a little sharp or flat, and watch live pitch feedback while you practise.

Find drone sounds for all the keys you play.

Choose drones for D, C, A, Bb and more. Pick between calm or dramatic character and build intonation, tone and colour over a steady reference.

Keep perfect timing with the metronome.

Set the tempo with tap, choose the feel and sound, and keep your timing solid while you work on ornamentation and long phrases.

Study recordings in detail with the audio lab.

Load a track, trim the phrase you want, slow it down, transpose and loop – ideal for learning by ear, one phrase at a time.

Exercises that give your practice a plan.

Guided exercises for breathing, tone endurance, vibrato and scales help you turn “noodle time” into focused, structured practice.

Care advice for your duduks and reeds.

Learn how to soak, adjust and test reeds, store and protect your instrument, and troubleshoot common problems – without getting lost in random forum threads.

FAQ

Is DudukPro only for duduk?

Yes. The app is designed specifically for the Armenian duduk, with tools and content tailored to this instrument.

What language is the app in?

The app interface is in English. This website and the store listing provide translations where helpful.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Once installed, DudukPro works fully offline. You only need a connection to download the app or updates.

Is DudukPro a replacement for a teacher?

No. DudukPro is a practice companion – it helps you make your own practice time more focused and less frustrating, alongside real teachers and musicians.
