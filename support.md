# DudukPro – Support

Welcome to the support page for **DudukPro**.

DudukPro is an all-in-one practice companion built specifically for the Armenian duduk. It brings together tools for pitch, rhythm, audio study, exercises and care, all in one quiet place.

---

## Contact

If you need help, have found a bug, or want to request a feature, you can reach me at:

- **Email:** dudukpro@markusdrayss.com  

When you contact me about a problem, it really helps if you include:

- Your device model (e.g. iPhone 13)  
- Your iOS version (e.g. iOS 18.1)  
- What you were trying to do  
- What happened instead (and any error messages)

---

## Common Questions

**I found a bug. What should I do?**  
Please email me with the details listed above. Screenshots or short screen recordings are very welcome if you can provide them.

**I have an idea for a new feature.**  
I’d love to hear it. Tell me how you use the app and what would make your duduk practice easier or more enjoyable.

**I have a question about privacy.**  
You can read the full privacy policy here:  
[View the DudukPro Privacy Policy](https://periboo.github.io/dudukpro/privacy.html)

If you still have questions or concerns after reading it, feel free to contact me using the email above.
